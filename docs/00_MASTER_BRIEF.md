# 00 — Master Brief

## Objective

Rebuild La Maison Estética's public website as a premium, modern and conversion-focused digital experience. Preserve useful business information from the current public site, but reorganize and rewrite it for clarity, confidence and action.

## Business goal

Increase qualified appointment inquiries, primarily through WhatsApp and a future booking flow, while improving perceived quality, trust and brand value.

## Primary audience

People in CABA and nearby areas considering facial, body or beauty treatments who value:

- natural-looking results;
- professional guidance;
- privacy and personalized attention;
- a premium environment;
- simple contact and booking.

## Positioning

La Maison should feel like a contemporary boutique aesthetic studio with professional credibility. The experience should balance warmth, beauty and restraint.

Desired perception:

- premium but approachable;
- sophisticated but not pretentious;
- aesthetic but not childish;
- professional but not hospital-like;
- feminine-neutral rather than aggressively pink.

## Core message hierarchy

1. What La Maison does.
2. Why the experience is different.
3. Which treatments are available.
4. Why a visitor should trust the brand.
5. How to request an evaluation or appointment.

## Conversion actions

Primary CTA: `Reservar una evaluación` or the approved business equivalent.

Secondary CTA: `Hablar por WhatsApp`.

Supporting CTA: `Ver tratamientos`.

Do not create multiple competing CTA labels. Use one consistent vocabulary throughout the site.

## Recommended page structure

1. Header and navigation.
2. Hero with clear value proposition and primary CTA.
3. Compact trust/value strip using only verified statements.
4. Featured treatments.
5. Brand philosophy / personalized approach.
6. Complete treatment categories.
7. Results or gallery only with approved assets and consent.
8. Team or professional section only with verified names and credentials.
9. Process: consultation, recommendation, treatment, follow-up.
10. Testimonials only if verified.
11. FAQ.
12. Location, hours, contact and map.
13. Final CTA.
14. Footer with legal and social links.

## Copy principles

- Spanish for Argentina.
- Clear, short sentences.
- Avoid exaggerated promises and generic luxury clichés.
- Prefer `resultados naturales`, `evaluación personalizada`, `acompañamiento` and `bienestar` only where supported.
- Avoid fear-based messaging.
- Avoid medical claims unless verified.

## Success criteria

- The value proposition is understood within five seconds.
- Primary CTA appears above the fold on mobile and desktop.
- Text meets accessible contrast.
- Treatments are easy to scan and filter mentally.
- Contact details are consistent across the page.
- Mobile navigation and WhatsApp access are effortless.
- Lighthouse targets: Performance 90+, Accessibility 95+, Best Practices 95+, SEO 95+ where realistic.
